Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 2026

At is-app.top, we are committed to transparency. Because this is an open-source project powered by GitHub, your data is handled differently than traditional services.

1. Data Collection

We do not require account registration on our website. However, data is collected when you interact with our GitHub repository:

  • GitHub Username: To identify the owner of the subdomain.
  • DNS Records: Technical data (IP addresses, CNAME targets) provided in your Pull Request.
  • Public Profile Info: Any information you have set to "Public" on GitHub.

2. Public Registry Disclosure

Warning: This service operates as a Public Registry. When you submit a Pull Request to register a subdomain:

  • Your technical records become permanently public on our GitHub repository.
  • Commit history will reflect all changes made to your records.

3. Data Usage

Your data is used solely to:

  • Operate and maintain the DNS infrastructure for your subdomain.
  • Communicate regarding technical issues or Terms of Service violations.
  • Prevent abuse, phishing, or malicious activities.

4. Third-Party Services

We utilize the following infrastructure:

  • GitHub: For storage and management of domain records.
  • Cloudflare: For DNS resolution and infrastructure security.

These services may collect technical logs (like IP addresses) independently under their own privacy policies.

5. Your Rights

You may update or request the removal of your subdomain at any time by opening a Pull Request. Note that while current records can be deleted, historical data may persist in the Git commit history.
